About.

SHACKLES 2025 is the annual state-level technical symposium organized by Mechasso, the Mechanical Engineering Association of Alagappa Chettiar Government College of Engineering & Technology (ACGCET), Karaikudi. Held over two days in February or March, this prestigious event attracts participants from various colleges across the state, fostering innovation, technical excellence, and collaboration. ACGCET, established in 1952, is a government autonomous engineering college in Karaikudi, Tamil Nadu. It is among the 11 institutions in Tamil Nadu selected under the TEQIP (Technical Education Quality Improvement Programme), reflecting its commitment to academic excellence and technological advancement.

Developed and Maintained by Mech Asso ACGCET
